Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, often referred to simply as CR7, is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Known for his incredible speed, agility, and goal-scoring prowess, Ronaldo has achieved immense success throughout his career, winning numerous domestic and international titles. He is known for his exceptional work ethic, dedication to training, and leadership both on and off the field.

Born: 5 February 1985 (age 39 years), Funchal, Madeira, Portugal

Height: 1.87 m

Spouse: Georgina Rodríguez (Partner)

Children: Cristiano Ronaldo Jr., Eva Maria Dos Santos, Mateo Ronaldo, Alana Martina dos Santos Aveiro

Parents: José Dinis Aveiro, Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro
