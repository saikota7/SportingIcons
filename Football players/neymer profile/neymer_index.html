Neymar Jr

Neymar Jr., often simply referred to as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer known for his exceptional dribbling skills, flair, and goal-scoring ability. He has established himself as one of the most prominent players in the world, having won numerous domestic and international titles. Neymar is known for his agility, creativity, and ability to change the course of a game with his individual brilliance.

Born: 5 February 1992 (age 32 years), Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil

Height: 1.75 m

Children: Davi Lucca da Silva Santos

Parents: Neymar Santos Sr., Nadine Gonçalves
