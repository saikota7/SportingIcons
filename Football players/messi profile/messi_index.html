Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, often considered one of the greatest footballers of all time, is an Argentine professional footballer known for his extraordinary dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability. Messi has spent the majority of his career with Barcelona, where he has won numerous domestic and international titles, including multiple Ballon d'Or awards for the best player in the world. He is renowned for his low center of gravity, quick acceleration, and precise finishing.

Born: 24 June 1987 (age 36 years), Rosario, Argentina

Height: 1.70 m

Spouse: Antonela Roccuzzo (m. 2017)

Children: Thiago Messi, Mateo Messi, Ciro Messi

Parents: Jorge Messi, Celia María Cuccittini
