Steve Smith

Steve Smith is an Australian international cricketer known for his remarkable batting technique and consistency across all formats of the game. He is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world and has achieved numerous records in Test cricket. Smith is known for his unorthodox yet effective batting style, remarkable hand-eye coordination, and ability to adapt to various conditions.

Born: 2 June 1989 (age 34 years), Sydney, Australia

Height: 1.78 m

Spouse: Dani Willis (m. 2018)

Parents: Peter Smith, Gillian Smith
