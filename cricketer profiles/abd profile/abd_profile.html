AB de Villiers

AB de Villiers, also known as Abraham Benjamin de Villiers, is a former South African international cricketer widely regarded as one of the most versatile and innovative batsmen of his generation. He is known for his unorthodox batting style, exceptional fielding skills, and ability to play a wide range of shots all around the ground. De Villiers is often referred to as "Mr. 360" due to his ability to play shots in all directions.
