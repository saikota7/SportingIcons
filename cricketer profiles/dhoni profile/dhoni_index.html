MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and one of the most successful cricket captains in history. He is a right-handed middle-order batsman and wicket-keeper known for his cool and calm demeanor on the field, earning him the nickname "Captain Cool." Dhoni is renowned for his astute leadership, finishing abilities, and tactical acumen.

Born: 7 July 1981 (age 42 years), Ranchi

Children: Ziva Dhoni

Spouse: Sakshi Dhoni (m. 2010)

Height: 1.75 m

Parents: Pan Singh, Devaki Devi
