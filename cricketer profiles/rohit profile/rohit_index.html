Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer known for his exceptional batting skills, particularly in limited-overs cricket. He is a right-handed batsman and occasional right-arm off-break bowler, regarded as one of the best opening batsmen in the world. Sharma holds numerous records in international cricket and has established himself as a key player in the Indian cricket team across all formats.

Born: 30 April 1987 (age 36 years), Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra

Height: 1.73 m

Spouse: Ritika Sajdeh (m. 2015)

Children: Samaira Sharma

Parents: Gurunath Sharma, Purnima Sharma
